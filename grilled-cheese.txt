Ingredients
1. Bread
2. Cheese
3. Butter

Instructions
1. Heat skillet over medium heat
2. Once the skiller is hot, smear butter on skillet
3. Get 2 slices of bread and put some cheese in between them
4. Place sandwhich on skillet
5. Using a spatula or plate press down on the sandwich
6. After 1 minute, lift sandwhich to check if bread is toasted
7. Once the bread is the toasted to your preference, flip the sandwhich
